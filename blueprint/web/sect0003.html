3 Banach Energy Space Framework

3.1 Abstract Framework

Definition 44 Banach energy space

BanachEnergySpace The abstract Banach energy space with pairings, divergence \(\delta \), and expectation.

Definition 45 Centering

BanachEnergySpace.center def:banach_space \(\mathrm{center}(G_0) = G_0 - \mathrm{const}(\mathbb {E}[G_0])\).

Theorem 46 Center is centered

BanachEnergySpace.center_centered def:center \(\mathbb {E}[\mathrm{center}(G_0)] = 0\).

3.2 The Derivative \(D\)

Definition 47 Operator-covariant derivative

BanachEnergySpace.D def:banach_space \(D(F) := \mathrm{mk\_ dual}(u \mapsto \mathbb {E}[F \cdot \delta (u)])\). Constructed, not axiomatized.

Theorem 48 \(D\) defining identity

BanachEnergySpace.D_def def:D \(\langle DF, u \rangle = \mathbb {E}[F \cdot \delta (u)]\).

Theorem 49 \(D\) linear

BanachEnergySpace.D_linear thm:D_def \(D(\alpha F + G) = \alpha DF + DG\).

Theorem 50 \(D\) on constants

BanachEnergySpace.D_const thm:D_def \(D(\mathrm{const}(c)) = 0\).

Theorem 51 \(\ker (D)\) characterization

BanachEnergySpace.ker_D_iff_annihilates_im_delta thm:D_def \(DF = 0\) iff \(F\) annihilates \(\mathrm{im}(\delta )\).

3.3 Fluctuation Factorization (Theorem A)

Theorem 52 Fluctuation factorization

BanachEnergySpace.fluctuation_factorization def:D, def:banach_space \((F - \mathbb {E}F) = \delta (\delta ^{-1}(F - \mathbb {E}F))\) on \(\mathrm{im}(\delta )\).

Theorem 53 Duality recovery

BanachEnergySpace.duality_recovery thm:factorization, thm:D_def \(\langle D F, u \rangle \) recovers the integrand.

3.4 Representability Obstruction

Definition 54 Distributional symmetry

BanachEnergySpace.DistributionalSymmetry def:banach_space \(L \stackrel{d}{=} -L\): negation map on \(L^q\).

Theorem 55 Even annihilates \(\mathrm{im}(\delta )\)

BanachEnergySpace.even_annihilates_im_delta def:dist_sym, thm:D_def If \(G\) is even, \(\mathbb {E}[G \cdot \delta (u)] = 0\) for all \(u\).

Theorem 56 Representability obstruction

BanachEnergySpace.representability_obstruction thm:even_annihilates, thm:center_centered Even, centered, nonzero \(G\) \(\Rightarrow \) \(G \notin \mathrm{im}(\delta )\).

3.5 Leibniz Defect (Theorem B)

Definition 57 Leibniz defect

BanachEnergySpace.leibniz_defect def:D \(\Gamma (F,G) := D(FG) - F \cdot DG - G \cdot DF\).

Theorem 58 Product rule with jump defect

BanachEnergySpace.product_rule_with_jump_defect def:leibniz \(\langle \Gamma (F,G), u \rangle = \mathbb {E}[\sum \Delta _s(f,g) \cdot \delta (u)]\).

Theorem 59 Defect vanishes without jumps

BanachEnergySpace.defect_vanishes_without_jumps def:leibniz No jumps \(\Rightarrow \) \(\Gamma = 0\).

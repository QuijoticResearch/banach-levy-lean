Definition 44Banach energy space

A Banach integrand space \(H_X\) with dual \(H_X^*\), \(L^p\)–\(L^q\) function spaces, divergence \(\delta : H_X \to L^p\), duality pairings, and expectation. Unlike the Hilbert framework, no Riesz identification \(H_X^* \cong H_X\) is assumed.

Definition 22Constructed compensated integral

Definition 47Operator-covariant derivative
